The Philosophy
"Most skill galleries optimize for doing more, faster. We optimize for doing what matters — and automating the rest."
— The Mck-skills Manifesto
→
Move 1: Add Meaning
Hollow Victory → Purposeful Impact. Automate the mechanical (decks, formatting, boilerplate) and invest the freed time into projects you genuinely care about.
↘
Move 2: Step Off
Hollow Victory → Quiet Contentment. Sometimes you don't need another promotion. You need a walk without your phone, a book, a conversation about something other than work.
↗
Move 3: Find Structure
The Drift → Anywhere. When you're lost, use the same frameworks you'd apply to a client problem — but point them inward. Structure is salvation.
